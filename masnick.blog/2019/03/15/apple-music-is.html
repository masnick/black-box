Apple Music is still kind of weird

Mar 15, 2019

It’s awesome that Apple Music has a Deutsche Grammophon-curated “Browse” section, but there doesn’t appear to be a way to actually find this in the Apple Music iOS app.

You can search for “Deutsche Grammophon” but that only will bring you to this page.

The “Browse” page, which is at http://www.applemusic.com/dg (I didn’t know applemusic.com was a thing until now), does not appear to actually be discoverable through Apple Music. Maybe it magically appears in “Browse” for some users?

This is annoying because the “Browse” page has way more content than the search result for DG. Why in the world don’t they surface the “Browse” page for DG in search results?

Also when I tried going to applemusic.com on iOS, I was sent to the App Store and shown an error indicating that I need to update to at least iOS 8.4 to use Apple Music (I’m on iOS 12.1.4).

Update:

https://music.apple.com/us/curator/deutsche-grammophon/1099882563 works at least on macOS.
